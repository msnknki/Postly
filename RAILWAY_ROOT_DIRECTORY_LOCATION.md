## Location: "Build" Section

The **Root Directory** setting is located in the **"Build"** category.

### Steps:

1. **In Railway Settings tab**, look at the right sidebar
2. **Click on "Build"** (in the quick links on the right)
3. **Scroll down** in the Build section
4. You should see **"Root Directory"** setting
5. **Click to edit** or "Add Root Directory"
6. **Enter:** `server`
7. **Save**

---

## Alternative: Check "Deploy" Section

If not in "Build", check the **"Deploy"** section:

1. **Click on "Deploy"** in the right sidebar
2. Look for **"Root Directory"** or **"Working Directory"**
3. Set it to: `server`

---

## What to Look For

In the Build or Deploy section, you should see settings like:
